Latenza Script is a flowing calligraphic script with a soft, composed rhythm.

The letters move with long strokes and gentle turns, giving the text a calm confidence rather than a loud flourish. It carries a classic feeling, but with relaxed proportions that make it feel natural and contemporary. The connections are smooth and intentional, allowing words to breathe while still holding together as a unified line.

The font is built with advanced OpenType functionality and guaranteed top-notch quality, containing stylistic and contextual alternates, ligatures and more automatic and manual features; all to give you full control and customizability. 
It has extensive lingual support, covering all Latin-based languages, from North Europa to South Africa, from America to South-East Asia. It contains all characters and symbols you'll ever need, including all punctuation and numbers. 

Designed by Måns Grebäck, Latenza Script focuses on balance and readability within a handwritten form. The design favors clarity and elegance, letting the details support the text instead of overpowering it.
